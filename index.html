About

This website was built as a lightweight tool to quickly generate QR codes for free from any link. It’s a small personal project designed to make QR creation fast, easy, and accessible to everyone - no complicated setup or unnecessary features.

Whether you're creating a QR code for a business card, event poster, or social media profile, this tool helps you do it in just a few clicks. Simply paste your link, and your QR code is ready to download and share.

The goal of this project is to provide a smooth, user-friendly experience for anyone who needs a QR code - anytime, anywhere.
Best of all, it's completely free to use.

FAQ

Q: What is a QR code used for?

A: QR codes are used to quickly share information like website links, contact details, Wi-Fi passwords, or app downloads. They're commonly found on flyers, packaging, menus, business cards, and more.

Q: Is it free to generate QR codes on this site?

A: Yes! This tool is completely free to use — generate as many QR codes as you need.

Q: Do the QR codes created here expire?

A: No. The QR codes you generate here are static, meaning they do not expire and will always point to the link you entered.

Q: Can i customize the QR code design?

A: Currently, this tool generates simple black-and-white QR codes. In the future, we may add design options like colors, logos, or shapes.

Q: Is my data or link stored somewhere?

A: No, we don’t store or track any links you enter. Your QR code is generated instantly and privately in your browser.

Q: Do I need to create an account to use this tool?

A: Nope — no registration or sign-in required. Just paste your link and download your QR code.
